About Charipay

Charipay is a charity and campaign platform designed to connect people with meaningful causes and make giving more accessible, transparent, and impactful.

Our Mission

Charipay aims to make charitable giving simple and trustworthy by helping people discover active campaigns, understand the needs behind them, and support causes that matter.

Our goal is to create a platform where generosity can meet real community needs through a clear and user-friendly experience.

What Charipay stands for
  • Making donations more accessible
  • Helping users discover meaningful campaigns
  • Encouraging transparency in charitable support
  • Building trust through a simple digital experience

What You Can Do on Charipay

A simple platform experience built around causes, campaigns, and support.

Browse Campaigns

Explore active campaigns, view their goals, and understand the purpose behind each cause.

Donate with Confidence

Support campaigns through a simple donation experience with clear campaign information.

Create Positive Impact

Help communities by contributing to campaigns that aim to make a real difference.

Our Vision

Charipay is being built with the vision of becoming a trusted digital platform for charitable giving, where people can easily support campaigns, discover important causes, and help create stronger communities through accessible technology.
