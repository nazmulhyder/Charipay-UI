About Charipay

Charipay is a UK-first charity and campaign platform designed to connect people with meaningful causes and make giving simple, transparent, and impactful.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make charitable giving more accessible and trustworthy by helping people discover active campaigns, understand the real needs behind them, and contribute with confidence.

Starting with UK-based campaigns, Charipay focuses on building a clear and user-friendly experience where generosity can directly support communities and meaningful initiatives.

What Charipay stands for
  • Making donations simple and accessible
  • Helping people discover meaningful campaigns
  • Promoting transparency in charitable support
  • Building trust through a clean and reliable platform

What You Can Do on Charipay

A simple experience built around campaigns, contributions, and community impact.

Browse Campaigns

Explore active campaigns, understand their goals, and discover causes that matter to you.

Donate with Confidence

Support campaigns through a simple and secure donation experience, with all contributions processed in GBP (£).

Create Real Impact

Contribute to campaigns that aim to support communities and create meaningful, lasting change.

Our Vision

Charipay is being built with the vision of becoming a trusted digital platform for charitable giving — starting with UK-based campaigns and growing to support global communities. The goal is to make it easier for people to contribute, stay connected with causes, and create meaningful impact through accessible technology.
