How Charipay Works

Charipay supports two simple journeys: donors can browse campaigns and donate anonymously or through an account, while volunteers can find opportunities and support campaign activities.

For Donors

Support campaigns with confidence

Donate anonymously or create an account to track your giving.

Browse Campaigns

Explore UK-based campaigns and find causes that matter to you.

Choose How to Donate

Donate anonymously, or create an account to track your donation history.

Donate in GBP (£)

Make a simple and secure contribution to the selected campaign.

View Donation History

Logged-in donors can view previous donations and supported campaigns.

For Volunteers

Give your time to support causes

Find volunteering tasks, apply, and track your progress from start to completion.

Search Opportunities

Browse available volunteer opportunities linked to active campaigns.

Create Volunteer Account

Register as a volunteer to access task details and apply for opportunities.

Check Task Details

Review the specific volunteering task, schedule, location, and requirements.

Apply and Complete

Apply for the task, start after approval, and mark progress through completion.

Choose how you want to help

Support campaigns financially as a donor, or give your time as a volunteer.
