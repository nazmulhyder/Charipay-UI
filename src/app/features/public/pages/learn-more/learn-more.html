Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to donate?

Charipay supports both anonymous and non-anonymous donation flows, depending on the feature setup.

Can I support a campaign anonymously?

Yes, Charipay is designed to support anonymous donation options for users who prefer privacy.

How can I know whether a campaign is active?

Active campaigns display their current status, helping users understand whether they are available for support.

Will more features be added in the future?

Yes, Charipay is being developed further with the goal of adding richer campaign experiences, improved donation flows, and more user-focused features.
